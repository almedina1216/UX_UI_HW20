Project Showcase

Friendship Circle of Colorado

I was part of the UX/UI experience design team working on a redesign for the site Friendship Circle of Colorado. FCOC is a nonprofit organization which connects children and teens with disabilities with peer volunteers to provide a social connection. FCOC offers social opportunities to promote equality, confidence, and opportunity.
