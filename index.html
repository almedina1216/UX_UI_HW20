Project Showcase

Friendship Circle of Colorado

I was part of the UX/UI experience design team working on a redesign for the site Friendship Circle of Colorado. FCOC is a nonprofit organization which connects children and teens with disabilities with peer volunteers to provide a social connection. FCOC offers social opportunities to promote equality, confidence, and opportunity.

Tools:


Figma,Figjam,Miro,Trello

My Role:


UX UI design

My Team:


Jen Cristner
Michael Powel

Overview:

We Were tasked with finding a non-profit that could benifit from a redesign. We decided to work with Friendship Circle of Colorado because of their compeling mission to promote friendship and inclusivity for children with disabilities.

Understanding the Problem:

At the beginning of the project our team broke down the user research to test the current website design and define the problem. We found the navigation was confusing and that users could not fing how to RSVP for an event.

Solution:

Our team decided to focus on redesigning the navigation and the RSVP process. We thought adding clarity to these two steps could improve community involvment which was the stakeholders main goal.

The Process:

As a team we conducted a card sorting activity to determine the best information archatecture for the site.

User Flow:

Our next step was to determine the user flow for creating an RSVP for an event.

Mid-Fidelity Wireframes:

With the user flow established we were able to move on to creating mid-fidelity wireframes for our next round of testing.

Prototype Testing:

Take aways from testing

  • Navigation text needed to be resized on the desktop verstion
  • Users thought the form was unclear when adding more then one child to the same text box
  • When creating an RSVP users requested an add to calendar feature
  • Buttons labels were redundant and confusing these needed to be redesigned

Reflection:

This design allowed me to experience working with stakeholders.I learned how vital it is to have clear communication in order to address all of the stakeholders needs.I also learned how important it is to complete testing at each step of the design process.Our team got the opportunity to improve on the design each time we gained knowledge from our user feedback.
